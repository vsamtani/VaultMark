Local File Encryption and Protective Marking — Without the Cloud

VaultMark is the simplest way to encrypt, decrypt, and mark files securely — entirely within your browser, with no uploads, no logins, and no data leaving your device. Completely free and open source.

  • Strong Encryption: Encrypt or decrypt any file type with a clean, intuitive interface.
  • Native ZIP & PDF Support: No custom formats, no surprises.
  • Protective PDF Marking: Apply classification stamps and sensitivity labels easily.
  • 100% Offline Processing: Files never leave your device.
  • Free and Open Source: Built transparently for trust and collaboration.
